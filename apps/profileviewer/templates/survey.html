Along with the GeoExpert Judging System, we made this survey because we also want to know more about the decision making process when you judge one as an expert or not. To be more specific, we would like to know your criteria and they are important for us to consider for our future systems.

In this survey, we also provide some hints on judging an expert. However, ONLY when you REALLY think they are sound, should you take them into account in your future judgement. You can safely ignore them and insist on your way of judging experts.
