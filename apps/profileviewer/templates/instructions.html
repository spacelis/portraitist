Welcome

We are conducting a research on finding experts who can give suggestions about where to go. It is great that you come and help us evaluate the candidates.

Basically you will look into the profile of the user depicted in a bunch of charts. These charts show the distribution of places the user have been to. For example the chart on the right shows that the user have been to a lot of food places.

Besides the distribution over various kinds of locations, we also provide a bar chart displaying a candidate visiting timeline. It shows how many times user visited a place in a week.

The map can render a candidate's foot print around different places.

The interface also features interactivity.

When you click on a pie slice or drag mouse over the timeline, other charts will update to show only the select range of visits.

To help you quickly get to interesting parts of users' visits, a list of recommended filters are provided. Clicking on these buttons will make the interface focus on the selected visits, just like clicking on a pie slice. Do not fully rely on the recommended filters, they may not be sufficient for a judgement.

If you having trouble of restoring the states of charts, you may always click the reset button.

After your investigation, you assign a score to their expertise on a scale from 1 (Novice) to 5 (Expert). We sincerely appreciate your work!

Pro tip: When you are confused about a chart or a term, try hover your mouse over it.
